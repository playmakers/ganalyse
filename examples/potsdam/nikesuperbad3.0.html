Better and badder than ever - The Nike® Superbad Receiver Gloves boast a lightweight, versatile design that speed-hungry players demand. Engineered with a stretchy fit and innovative Supergrip® Technology, the Men's Superbad 3.0 Football Gloves ensure Nike's® tackiest, most dependable grip ever. Compression-molded padding is strategically layered on the knuckles and back-of-hand for added protection.

FEATURES:

• Men's football receiver gloves
• Lightweight, versatile design built specifically for adult skill players
• Supergrip® palm technology ensures Nike's® tackiest, all-weather grip
• Engineered with a stretchy fit for added comfort and a lock-down feel
• Pre-curved fingers help reduce palm bunching for increased grip surface area
• Integrated contoured padding on the knuckles and back-of-hand for protection
• Dual-Tension molded straps offer a lock-down fit and feel while reducing bunching
• Articulated thumbs enhance dexterity and overall feel
• Versatile wear options allow players to lock down the wrist strap or flip up the cuff
• Approved for NFHS® and NCAA® play
• External jock tag on wrist cuff lets you write your name or number
