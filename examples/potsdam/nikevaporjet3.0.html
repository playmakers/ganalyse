Artikelbeschreibung
 

Engineered to match the speed, skill and intensity of your game, the Nike® Vapor Jet 3.0 Receiver Gloves deliver a lightweight feel and superior mobility without sacrificing toughness. The Men's Vapor Jet 3.0 Football Gloves feature Magnigrip CL palms with eye-catching Nike® graphics for superior tack and grip no matter what the weather dishes out. Dual-Tension molded wrist straps offer a lock-down fit and feel with reduced bunching.

FEATURES:


Men's football receiver gloves
Tough and lightweight design built specifically for adult skill players
Ventilated mesh panels add breathability and lightweight comfort across the back-of-hand
Magnigrip CL palms with Nike® graphics for superior tack and grip in all conditions
Dual-Tension molded wrist straps offer a lock-down fit and feel while reducing bunching
Articulated thumbs enhance dexterity and overall feel
Versatile wear options allow players to lock down the wrist strap or flip up the cuff
External jock tag on wrist cuff lets you write your name or number
Meets NOCSAE® standard for NFHS® and NCAA®
