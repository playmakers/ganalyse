Cutters S60 The ShockSkin Gamer

Art. nr. 56220015

Cutters has teamed with Shock Doctor to incorporate ShockSkin™ Contour Technology into The Gamer, taking the all-purpose streamlined glove to the next level. This glove is for players looking for low profile protection with the feel, grip and dexterity of a top receivers glove. Thin, lightweight dual-density athletic EVA foam with strategically designed ergonomic flex channels provides unrestricted freedom of movement and comfort. Features a full C-TACK palm for amazing grip and durability. Ideal for virtually any position: WR, RB, TE, OL, DL, LB, DB.
